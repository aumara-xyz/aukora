# Claims Boundary

## What Aukora Claims

- Post-quantum cryptographic identity (ML-DSA-65, hybrid Ed25519+ML-DSA-65)
- Consumed-authority reference monitor (one-shot authority consumption)
- Multi-model consensus with geometry-informed verdicts (KL-divergence + phase-lock)
- Hardened immune gate (9-projection Unicode secret scanner, 146 tests)
- Owner-consented self-modification governance
- Deterministic O(n) credential scanning with step-budget proof
- Advisory-only, fail-closed architecture

## What Aukora Does NOT Claim

- General AI or AGI capabilities
- Consciousness, sentience, or aliveness
- Biological isomorphism or topology in production
- Autonomous authority (all authority requires owner consent)
- Deployment or operational guarantees
- Security beyond the tested boundary
