## Naming rules for future documents

1. Say **KIRA** when you mean the brain/memory law; say **Alien Core** when you mean the research
   wave; write **KIRAC** only as informal shorthand and never in CLAIMS/anatomy.
2. A research document may cite the verified surface by link; it may not restate verified claims in
   its own words (restatement drifts).
3. Every capability statement in this directory carries one label — VERIFIED (with a link out),
   RESEARCH_CANDIDATE, UNPROVEN, or FALSIFIED — enforced in part by the `verify:no-overclaim` gate.
